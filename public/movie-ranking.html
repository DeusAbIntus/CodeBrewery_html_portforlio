The Best Movies According to Nitish

My top 3 Movies of all-time

Ramayan : The legend of Prince Ram

Ramayan is a classic animated film that brings the epic tale of Lord Ram to life. The animation is stunning, and the storytelling is both engaging and educational. It beautifully captures the essence of the Ramayana, making it a must-watch for all ages.

Harry Potter Franchise

The Harry potter film series explores a magical world full of wonder and amazement. Children along with adults have fell in love with the wizarding world brought to us by J.K. Rowling

Bahubaali

Bahubaali is a cinematic masterpiece that combines stunning visuals with an epic storyline. The film's grandeur and scale are unmatched, making it a landmark in Indian cinema. The characters are well-developed, and the action sequences are breathtaking.
